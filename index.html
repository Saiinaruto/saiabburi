Food Item 2

Dosa: Dosa is a popular South Indian delicacy and a staple of South Indian cuisine. It is a thin, crispy pancake made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram). Dosas can be served with a variety of accompaniments, such as coconut chutney, sambar (a spicy lentil soup), and different types of vegetable curries. There are many variations of dosas, including masala dosa, paper dosa, and more.

Food Item 3

Idli: Idli is another iconic South Indian dish. It is a steamed rice cake made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal. Idlis are soft, spongy, and have a slightly tangy flavor due to the fermentation process. They are often served with coconut chutney, sambar, or tomato chutney. Idlis are a healthy and versatile dish, enjoyed as breakfast or a snack.
